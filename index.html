Hello, world

My name is Tien. I was born in Pleiku city, a city in central Vietnam located in the Central Highlands region. At 18 years old, I was awarded a full-ride scholarship as a Global Scholar at Babson College, where I’m currently pursuing my undergrad in Business Analytics & Information Technology Management.

I grew up in a traditional conservative Vietnamese society. Even though I always loved building things since small, I never thought I would get my hands dirty on technology, a field that was very male dominated at the time. I was proudly raised by women, and all they hoped for me was to have a stable job and get married.

I wrote the very first “Hello, world” when I was in my sophomore year. Since then, I was drawn into using technologies to build products that can improve people’s lives. Over 3 years, I have travelled and worked in the US, Vietnam, China and Singapore in product-related positions, learning more and more about technology. I self-taught myself how to code and other data tools whenever I have time.

Realizing how powerful technology is, I founded SheCodes Vietnam to empower more women learning about tech. Over 3 years, we established a community of 10,000+ were backed by a Vietnamese Shark tank investor - madam Lien.

I want to continue building products, and currently looking for PM roles in the US.
